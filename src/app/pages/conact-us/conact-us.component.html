Architectural Designer

Are you a creative thinker with a passion for transforming concepts into captivating architectural designs? We're seeking an Architectural Designer to join our team of visionaries.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with project teams to develop innovative design solutions.
  • Create detailed architectural plans, drawings, and 3D models.
  • Conduct site visits and assessments.
  • Ensure designs comply with local building codes and regulations.
  • Present design proposals to clients and stakeholders.
  • Stay updated on industry trends and best practices.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Architecture.
  • Proven experience in architectural design.
  • Proficiency in architectural software (AutoCAD, SketchUp, Revit, etc.).
  • Strong communication and presentation skills.
  • Attention to detail and a keen eye for aesthetics.
  • Ability to work effectively in a collaborative team environment.
